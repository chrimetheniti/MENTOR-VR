# ⚓ MENTOR — A VR Experience of a 19th-Century Shipwreck

> *Step aboard the brig "Mentor" in 1802 and prove yourself as a navigator — using real historical instruments recovered from the wreck.*

A VR application developed as a Master's thesis at the **University of the Aegean** (MSc in Cultural Informatics & Communication, 2023), exploring how immersive technology can bring underwater cultural heritage to life.

---

## 🎮 About the Project

The **Mentor** was a British brig that sank off the island of Kythera, Greece in 1802 — carrying part of the Elgin Marbles. This VR experience reconstructs the ship and its historical context, placing the player in the role of a local navigator candidate being tested by the ship's captain.

Using authentic navigational instruments recovered from the wreck site, the player must complete **3 challenges** to earn a place in the crew.

---

## 🕹️ Gameplay

| Challenge | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| **1 — Course Plotting** | Use a navigational compass (*kompassos*) to chart a route and measure nautical miles |
| **2 — Finding Degrees** | Rotate the ship's wheel to find the correct bearing |
| **3 — Navigation Quiz** | Answer questions using a ship's compass and hourglass (*klepsydra*) |

All three instruments — the **hourglass, navigational compass, and ship's compass** — are modeled based on actual archaeological findings from the Mentor wreck site.

## 🛠️ Tech Stack

- **Engine:** Unity 3D
- **VR SDK:** SteamVR / OpenXR
- **Render Pipeline:** HDRP (High Definition Render Pipeline)
- **Language:** C#
- **Key Tools:** TextMesh Pro, custom spatial UI, NPC animation system
